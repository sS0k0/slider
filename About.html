About Slider: Built by fans, for fans.

Slider was created in January of 2021 by three friends who wanted a better system to rate their favourite shows. The site is focused on fan ratings and fans can rate shows based on categories with “Sliders”. As one of the world’s leading fan databases, Slider is a trusted source that will provide accurate and relevant fan services for ratings across specific categories. All in all, Slider is a site for avid bingers, stats nerds, and fans alike; there is something in Slider for everyone out there.

What are the Sliders?

Adjust your Sliders to assign priority to five categories: Writing, Plot, Art Style, Animation, and Music. These priorities will apply to the scores you see when you search for shows and when you see other peoples ratings! Feeling a vibe for a show with good music? Push your music Slider all the way up and see the top rated shows for music.
